How to participate

If you want one or more of your bots to play, send an e-mail to absurd.punter@yandex.com and list the names you want for your bots. We will send you back an e-mail with security tokens for each of the names.

After that you can connect to punt.absurd.pw:53400 by the same online protocol with the only alternation: during handshake you send {"me" : token} instead of {"me" : name}.

If you are using lamduct, pass the following arguments: 

    lamduct --game-hostname=punt.absurd.pw --game-port=53400 ./my_awesome_punter

You may setup up to 10 connections for each token simultaneously. There may be a pause between handshake and game start due to matchmaking (it may take seconds, minutes, or more).

If your token was compromised or lost, you may request a new token from the same e-mail address.

We're glad you're with us! Have fun!

Protocol changes

1. At handshake stage you send {"me": token} instead of {"me": name}.
You may receive {"you": name} or {"kicked": reason} in response.
In the first case you'll be send the registered bot name.
The second may appear if server accepts no more players or if your bot has too many simultaneous connections.

2. During the game if you are kicked due to invalid request, you'll be sent message: {"kicked": reason}.

3. State is not sent back to you, neither are any other keys you include into response.
